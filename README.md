# Premise

A group of broke young adults start a freelance monster hunting agency to pay the bills. DMC-like game with a customizable party instead of a customizable loadout.  
World-wise, I think of some sort of Urban fantasy setting. Give an excuse for the whole freelance monster hunter thing. Maybe close to early RWBY, but with actually good worldbuilding.

### Why

	The characters exist in an urban fantasy setting. They are dealing with an issue that is both rare and usually harmless. This is why no one more experienced is in their place. However everything quickly falls apart as the situation escalates very rapidly.  
	

# Lore Ideas

\-Magic: Becoming magic is a totally random event. It usually happens soon before birth, though it can happen at any point in life.  
\-Magic weapons: Can be of many natures. Explain individually for each weapon.  
\-Break: Limit break. Something that happens when a mage\[Magic person\] sustains a certain amount of physical trauma. It is the barrier of the soul literally breaking, the body being flooded with so much magical energy that it reconstitutes the mage's body. This gives an in lore explanation for the powerup and visual change the character’s get. Maybe it also slowly heals the player too, like the Break meter drains at the same rate the health bar refills.

# Mechanic ideas

#### Style/combat: 

Players should be incentivised to build combos and  increase their style score. So Combos should be easy to start. Maybe repeatedly doing one thing makes it harder to keep the score up. Since no character is a dedicated healer (Because that’s lame) maybe doing attacks has a life-steal effect. It should start off small but still good enough that even people like me, with terrible combo consistency, still benefit. But it should get better and better with higher style scores.

#### Traversal: 

There should be areas in different sections of the game that different characters can traverse. Just for funsies to show different stuff and encourage replaying sections.

#### Swapping:

Something to let the player swap characters. Like how in DMC you swap weapons. Or I guess it’s closer to something like Genshin Impact (yucky).   
	Weapon swapping so the player can achieve more combos in different ways.
